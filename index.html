My Story

Amarja is an energetic student from Pune who loves playing Kho-Kho and reading books. Every evening after school, she joins her friends on the playground and enjoys exciting Kho-Kho matches. The game teaches her teamwork, speed, and confidence. After returning home, Amarja spends time reading interesting books filled with adventures and knowledge. One day, she imagined herself as a brave explorer from her favorite story. Inspired by the book, she led her Kho-Kho team with determination and helped them win an important match. Through sports and reading, Amarja learns valuable lessons and enjoys every day with enthusiasm.
